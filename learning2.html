Computer Hardware



The purpose of computer hardware is to provide the physical components that enable a computer to function. These components can include the central processing unit (CPU), memory, storage devices, input/output (I/O) devices, and other supporting components.

Computer hardware is constantly evolving, with new technologies and components being developed to improve performance, increase efficiency, and reduce costs. The purpose of computer hardware is to provide the foundation for computing systems, enabling users to perform a wide variety of tasks, from simple word processing to complex data analysis and scientific simulations.
