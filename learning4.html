Online Safety



In today's world, where the internet has become an integral part of our daily lives, it is essential to be safe online. The internet has brought about numerous benefits, including easy communication, online shopping, and access to vast amounts of information. However, it also exposes us to various dangers, such as cybercrime, online harassment, identity theft, and cyberbullying

Being safe online means taking appropriate measures to protect our personal information and privacy from being exploited by malicious actors. Cybercriminals use various techniques such as phishing, malware, and social engineering to obtain sensitive information such as passwords, bank details, and other confidential information.

Cyberbullying, which involves using the internet to harass or intimidate others, has also become a significant problem, leading to mental health issues, social isolation, and even suicide.
